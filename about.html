Our Story

Tradition Meets Flavor

At Teptep Inasal, we bring the authentic taste of Filipino grilling to your table. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the beloved flavors of traditional chicken inasal and classic silog meals with our community.

Each piece of chicken is marinated for hours in our signature blend of calamansi, lemongrass, and spices, then grilled over hot coals to achieve that perfect char and smoky aroma. Our silog meals honor the Filipino breakfast tradition, served all day for whenever hunger strikes.

Why We Started

Teptep Inasal was born out of a deep love for Filipino cuisine and a desire to share it with the world. Our founders, inspired by the rich flavors and traditions of their homeland, set out to create a restaurant that would not only serve delicious food but also bring people together through great hospitality.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect with others and celebrate the culture that surrounds us. That's why we take pride in using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients and preparing each dish with care and passion.
